Message from the Secretary General

Dear Delegates and Faculty Advisors,

It is my honour to welcome you to REQMUN 3.0, where students come together to debate, question, and engage with some of the most pressing global issues of our time.

My name is Rohan Arun, and my journey in Model UN began with simple curiosity and slowly grew into a deep passion for diplomacy, leadership, and collaboration. Over time, MUN became more than a platform for debate; it became a space where I learned the value of listening, thinking critically, and building solutions with others.

At its heart, REQMUN 3.0 stands for the idea that diplomacy is not only about speaking well, but about understanding perspectives and working together even in disagreement. This year, we aim to create a conference that is inclusive, intellectually challenging, and meaningful for delegates at every level of experience.

We warmly invite you to join us for REQMUN 3.0.

Regards,

Rohan Arun

Secretary-General, REQMUN 2026

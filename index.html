Message from the Secretary General

Dear Delegates, Faculty Advisors, and Distinguished Guests,

It is my distinct honor and privilege to welcome you to Reqelford Model United Nations 2026. In an era marked by rapid global shifts, complex geopolitical dynamics, and emerging technological frontiers, the art of diplomacy and collaborative dialogue has never been more critical to our collective future.

Reqelford MUN serves as a premier crucible for leadership, critical analysis, and intellectual exchange. This year, our conference is designed to push the boundaries of standard debate. As student delegates representing diverse sovereign interests, you will confront intricate global crises, draft progressive policies, and negotiate resolutions that demand both strategic foresight and empathetic compromise.

I urge you to approach each committee session with rigorous academic preparation, open minds, and an unwavering commitment to finding consensus. May your diplomatic journeys here inspire you to become the visionary leaders of tomorrow.

Sincerely,

Alex Carter

Secretary-General, RMUN 2026
